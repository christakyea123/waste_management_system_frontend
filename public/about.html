About Us

Building a Cleaner Ghana,
One Pickup at a Time

035 F Arkoh Waste Management is a Ghanaian-owned company on a mission to make professional, reliable waste collection accessible to every household and business — through technology, accountability, and people who care about the communities they serve.

Our Story

From a single truck to a national platform

We started 035 F Arkoh Waste Management because we kept seeing the same problem across our neighbourhoods: bins overflowing, missed pickups, and customers with no easy way to pay or get answers. The systems available were either too informal to depend on or too rigid to fit how Ghanaians actually live.

So we built something different — a service that combines disciplined, on-the-ground waste collection with the kind of digital tools customers expect: online registration, mobile-money payments, SMS notifications, and real visibility into every pickup.

Today, we serve households, businesses, and entire estates across Greater Accra and Ashanti, and we're growing every month. The truck fleet has grown. The team has grown. The mission hasn't.

What Drives Us

Mission, Vision & Values

Three commitments that shape every route we run and every decision we make.

Our Mission

To deliver dependable, dignified waste management services to every Ghanaian community — using technology to make the experience simple, transparent, and affordable for the customer, and efficient for the operator.

Our Vision

A Ghana where clean streets aren't a privilege — they're the standard. We see waste management as essential public infrastructure, and we're building the platform that makes that standard achievable nationwide.

Our Values

  • Reliability — if we promise a pickup, it happens.
  • Transparency — clear pricing, clear schedules, clear receipts.
  • Respect — for our customers, our crews, and the environment.
  • Improvement — every route, every month, sharper than the last.
What We Do

End-to-end waste management

From the bin at your gate to the disposal site — we own the entire journey so you don't have to think about it.

Residential Pickup

Scheduled weekly or bi-weekly doorstep collection for homes, with SMS notifications before every pickup and digital receipts after.

Commercial & Estate

Custom schedules and bin sizes for offices, restaurants, shops, and gated communities — with a dedicated account contact.

Recycling Program

Sorted recycling collection to help households and businesses divert plastic, paper, and metal from landfill.

Organic Waste

Specialised pickup for garden and food waste, routed to composting partners instead of general disposal.

2. The service

We provide scheduled waste collection services to residential and commercial customers in Ghana, together with a customer dashboard for managing your account, viewing collection history, paying invoices, and submitting complaints. We may add, change, or discontinue features at our discretion.

We offer three standard plans — Basic, Standard, and Premium — each with a defined bin size and collection frequency. The current price for each plan is published on our website and applies to invoices generated after the price is set. Prices are quoted in Ghana Cedis (GHS) and are inclusive of any applicable taxes unless stated otherwise.

Bins remain the property of 035 F Arkoh Waste Management unless explicitly sold. You agree to keep your bin in a reasonable state, accessible at the pickup point on collection days, and to report any damage or loss promptly.

5. Collection schedule

  • Pickups are scheduled on your assigned collection day. You will receive an SMS reminder before each pickup.
  • You agree to place your bin at the agreed pickup point before the start of the collection day, and to remove it after collection.
  • Collection times can vary within the day depending on route, traffic, and weather conditions.
  • We may temporarily adjust schedules to account for public holidays, severe weather, or operational emergencies. We'll communicate changes via SMS where reasonably possible.

6. Missed pickups

A pickup may be marked as missed if:

  • The bin was not at the pickup point at the time of arrival;
  • Access to your property was blocked (locked gate, parked vehicles, etc.);
  • There was no one available where customer presence is required;
  • An unforeseen operational issue (road closure, breakdown) prevented the pickup.

You will be notified via SMS when a pickup is marked missed, with the reason. Where the missed pickup was caused by us, we will reschedule at no extra cost. Where it was caused by access issues at your end, you may request a rescheduled pickup, which may be subject to an additional callout fee at our discretion.

7. Billing & payment

  • The due date for each invoice is shown on the invoice itself — typically the 25th of the month unless we communicate otherwise.
  • Payment is processed via Paystack. Accepted channels include debit/credit card, mobile money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), and bank transfer.
  • A receipt is sent via SMS once payment is confirmed.
  • You are responsible for any bank or mobile-money charges your provider applies to the transaction.
  • Refunds for duplicate payments are processed within 7 business days of confirmation. Refunds for missed services are issued as account credit unless we agree otherwise.

8. Late payment & suspension

If an invoice is not paid by its due date:

  • The invoice is marked overdue and you will receive automated SMS reminders;
  • We may add a late fee, where lawful, to subsequent invoices;
  • If the invoice remains unpaid for more than 30 days, we may suspend pickups until the balance is cleared;
  • Persistent non-payment may result in termination of service and referral to a collections process.

9. Prohibited waste

For the safety of our crew and the integrity of disposal sites, the following items must not be placed in your bin:

  • Hazardous chemicals, paints, solvents, pesticides, or used oils;
  • Medical or clinical waste (sharps, used dressings, expired pharmaceuticals);
  • Asbestos, construction debris, large quantities of rubble or soil;
  • Batteries, fluorescent tubes, and electronic waste (e-waste);
  • Flammable, explosive, or radioactive materials;
  • Liquid waste (e.g. cooking oil in open containers), free-flowing food slurry;
  • Dead animals or animal carcasses;
  • Bin contents exceeding the lid-closeable line of your bin.

If prohibited waste is found, we may refuse the pickup, charge a special handling fee, or — in severe or repeated cases — terminate the account. Customers requiring special disposal should contact us to arrange a dedicated pickup.
